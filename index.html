Roberto's Ethos

I used to stick to a strict athletic training routine, but it ended up causing more harm than good. Following advice that didn't really fit me, I ended up hurting myself. The programs I followed just brought more problems. Then, I realized I needed to take charge of my own healing.

Now, I want to share what I've learned a more flexible, personalized approach to feeling and moving better. It's about finding hope and calmness in strength, step by step.

Let's journey together towards a healthier, happier you.

Online Classes

For the past three years, I've been hosting and leading an online movement class every Monday at 7:30 am.

It serves as a refreshing start to the week, encouraging participants to pause, reflect, and then embark on the journey of a new week together.

Following each session, participants receive a link to the video, allowing them to revisit and engage with the session at their own pace throughout the week.

About

I’m aiming to explore & reduce uncertainty as a crazy artist.

After almost 40 years of practice I arrive at a point where my focus is the longevity of the practice. Longevity is not only understood as training and moving for 50 years more, also understanding that your practice must bring you joy and help you in your daily life and that’s what I’m interested to share:
